# Part 5: Hands-On
## Topics 11–13 | ~40 minutes

---

## Exercise 1: Sign Up & First Message (10 min)

1. Open **claude.ai** → Sign up
2. Open **gemini.google.com** → Sign in with Google
3. Prompt: "I'm new here. Tell me one thing most people don't know you can do."
4. Prompt: "I have a job interview for marketing manager at a tech company. Give me 5 likely questions and draft strong answers."
5. Compare responses between Claude and Gemini

---

## Exercise 2: The AI Taste Test (15 min)

Same prompt into ChatGPT, Claude, AND Gemini. Pick one:
- **Option A:** Professional email — project 2 weeks late, apologize without groveling
- **Option B:** Lease vs buy — 20K km/yr, 5+ years, $500/mo budget
- **Option C:** Explain mortgage rates to a first-time buyer

Compare: Most helpful? Longest ≠ best? Who asked clarifying questions?

---

## Exercise 3: Upload Document + Image (15 min)

**Part A:** Upload a PDF/doc to Claude → "Summarize in 5 bullet points"
**Part B:** Photo of anything nearby → upload → "What's in this image?"
- Try: receipt splitting, error messages, plant ID, handwriting OCR
- This is where "fun toy" becomes "real tool"
